Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect a new bank account?

To connect a new bank account, go to the "Connect Bank" section in your dashboard. You'll need to provide your bank's name, account type, account number, and routing number. We use bank-level security to protect your information.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption and bank-level security to protect your data. We never store your bank credentials and only access read-only information with your permission.

How often is my data updated?

Your account data is updated in real-time. Transactions typically appear within minutes of being processed by your bank.

What should I do if I notice an unauthorized transaction?

If you notice any unauthorized transactions, immediately contact your bank to dispute the transaction. Then, report it to our support team through the contact form below so we can investigate.
